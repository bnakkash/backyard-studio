Backyard Studio

Gestures

  • One finger drag — pan the camera
  • Pinch — zoom
  • Two fingers twist — rotate around fingertip
  • Two fingers up / down — tilt camera
  • Double-tap — zoom in toward tap point

Items

  • Tap palette → tap yard to place
  • Tap an item to select · drag selected to move
  • Two-finger pinch / twist on selected — scale / rotate it
  • Long-press (mobile) or shift-click (desktop) — multi-select
  • Path items (Paver Trim, Stepping Stones) — tap waypoints, segments fill in

Yard boundary

  • Trace photo — upload an aerial, click corners
  • Walk — phone-based perimeter walk
  • Edit — drag existing corners

Keyboard (desktop)

  • ⌘Z undo · ⌘⇧Z redo · ⌘D duplicate
  • g toggle snap · Esc deselect · Del remove
  • Middle-mouse drag on a selected item — rotate it
