Trace Yard

Walk · Tap · Done

Walk the perimeter of your yard, tapping Mark Corner at each fence post. Your phone uses its compass and a step counter to figure out where each corner is relative to the others.

  1. Stand at any corner of your yard.
  2. Tap Start Tracing, then walk to the next corner.
  3. Tap Mark Corner at each one.
  4. When you walk back to the first corner, tap Close Loop.
Average adult: 2.5 ft. If you're tall, try 2.8. If your traced shape ends up too small or too big, this is the knob.
Best on iOS Safari. iOS will ask permission to read motion + orientation — that's the compass and step counter. We don't store or upload anything.
